Pasta

Combine the flour and salt in a large bowl. Add the eggs. Create a deep well in the middle of the flour and crack eggs into it. Combine the flour and eggs. ... Knead the dough. ... Rest the dough. ... Divide the dough. ... Begin rolling out the pasta. ... Thin the pasta.


Rissoto

Warm your broth. ... Sweat the shallot. ... Toast the rice. ... Deglaze the pan with wine. ... Slowly add the broth in increments, stirring in between. ... Continue adding broth until the rice is al dente and the broth is creamy. ... Finishing and serving the risotto
